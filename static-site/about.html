Teaching Philosophy

My approach to teaching yoga is centered around accessibility and personalization. I believe that yoga is for everybody and every body type. Each class is designed to meet students where they are, whether they're complete beginners or advanced practitioners.

I focus on creating a supportive environment where students can explore movement, breath, and mindfulness at their own pace. My classes emphasize proper alignment, breath awareness, and mindful movement to help students build strength, flexibility, and inner peace.
